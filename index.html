How to enter data from AMOS / LISREL / jamovi

1. Open your standardized solution output
2. Find "Standardized Regression Weights" (AMOS) or "Completely Standardized Solution" (LISREL)
3. Enter each construct, its dimensions (if any), and item loadings
4. Enter Cronbach's α from your reliability output at construct and dimension level
5. For correlations: use the inter-construct correlation matrix from your output

About HTMT approximation

HTMT is approximated here as r / √(AVE₁ × AVE₂). This is a conservative estimate. For exact HTMT, compute from the full item-level correlation matrix in R or jamovi.
